Who we are

♦

The Doulas Care Collective (DCC) is a group of birth professionals who offer quality care and evidence-based education for expectant parents and a space for doulas to build a strong sense of doula community. Through ongoing professional development, regular meetings for members, active mentoring, and a commitment to giving back to the community through educational workshops, our collective strives to create a close-knit community of doulas offering families professional doula support.



Childbirth education is designed to help women access accurate and up to date information about childbirth and make informed decisions about their care.


Doulas are trained to provide emotional and physical support to women and their partners during pregnancy, childbirth, and the postpartum period.


All newborns require essential newborn care to minimize the risk of illness and maximize their growth and development.


Breastfeeding does not always comes naturally. Wether it is external biases or physiological... having support is primordial. Lactation consultants provide education and management related to breastfeeding.


Our services include prenatal massage, birth and newborn photography, placenta encapsulation, professional counseling and therapies, yoga therapy, nutritional counseling, acupuncture, chiropractic adjustments, and so on.


"It takes a village to raise a child" but the village has to be strong too. It is why we believe that supporting one another is how we can encourage the families we serve to build their village.

We are so glad you found us! We know that welcoming a new member into your family can be both exciting and overwhelming at times. It is our goal that no matter where you are in your journey, you always feel right at home with the Doulas Care Collective (DCC). We are honored to be a part of this important chapter in your life and look forward to supporting you.

One of the big reasons the DCC was born is for us doulas to support one another, to be supported and support our doulas sisters. The more support we have, the better we can serve the families that hire us. Starting your doula practice, being on-call or even childcare can be very challenging but we don't have to do it alone. We are here and we are stronger together.

When it comes to pregnancy, birth and postpartum care, many professionals can be invloved. We are all in the same team, YOUR team. Here at the DCC, we thrive to build bridges a.k.a. a network of caring experts so we know where you can find the help you need.

When we support families, doulas and other perinatal professionals we also support our community because it is where our heart is. We are right here, close to you. We don't need to look very far to see individuals and families with the need to be supported and honestly we all need a little help and be held at times. Located in Broward and Palm Beach Counties, we have our community at heart.
